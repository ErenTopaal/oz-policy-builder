# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its Code of Conduct. The linked page is the authoritative text; we do
not maintain a forked copy here, so any future amendments published by the
Contributor Covenant maintainers do not silently diverge from our copy.

By participating in this project — issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any related community space — you agree to abide by that document.

All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Reporter
identity is kept confidential to the extent permitted by applicable law.

## Enforcement

Enforcement follows the
[Contributor Covenant Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines)
verbatim. Maintainers apply the four-step ladder (correction, warning,
temporary ban, permanent ban) at their discretion in proportion to the
violation.
